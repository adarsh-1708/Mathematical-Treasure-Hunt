Vashishtha Narayan Singh
2 April 1942 - 14 November 2019

Vashishtha Narayan Singh was an Indian Mathematician born on 2nd of April 1942 in Basantpur Bihar. He did his masters from Patna University. His questions during the class shocked the professors at Patna University as many of them were unsolved. The Principle of Patna challenged V.N. Singh to solve a mathematics quiz.The challenge was that if V.N singh passes the quiz he would be directly promoted to final year of masters or else he will have to be quiet in the classroom and not disturb his professors with his extraordinary questions. He was suffering from prolonged illness ,Schizophrenia. He disappeared during his train journey to Pune in 1989 and was found four years later in 1993 in Doriganj near Chhapra of Saran district to his student. This incident shocked the government and his further treatment was carried by the government Singh died on 14 November 2019 at Patna Medical College and Hospital in Patna after prolonged illness.
