George Cantor
3 March 1845- 6 January 1918

George Cantor was born on 3rd march 1845, in Halle Germany. Cantor was deeply interested into the field of Mathematics from his childhood. He has made major contributions to Pure Mathematics. He surprised his Professor at the age of 10 by performing the calculations of 1st 100 natural numbers easily . He performed these calculations by all the possible methods can be perform. He is widely known as the Founder of SET Theory. He also introduced the transfinite numbers. Cantor died by a fatal heart attack on January 6, 1918, in the sanatorium where he had spent the last year of his life
